"Where do I get/unlock/whatever..."

  • Unlock Savage Pandaemonium?
    • Aporia, Nemjiji @ 8.5, 27.3
  • 6.2 Normal Raid Weapon? (620 ilvl)
    • Run P8N at least once a week, for 7 weeks. You'll get "Unsung Blade of Abyssos", turn that in for an Ultralight Tomestone and with 500 Green Tomestones (Causality) for your unaugmented 620 weapon. You get the ultralight tomestone from your normal raid vendor, Djole in Radz-at-Han.

raid loot explained

You get one piece of gear per raid per week

various pieces of gear require various amounts of loot

  • Head Gear
    • you need 2 pieces of head loot
  • Chest Gear
    • you need 4 pieces of chest loot (one week's worth of loot)
  • Arm Gear
    • you need 2 pieces of arm loot
  • Leg Gear
    • you need 4 pieces of leg loot
  • Boot Gear
    • you need 2 pieces of boot loot
  • all accessories
    • it is 1:1.
    one ring = one jewelry
